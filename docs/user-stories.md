# User Stories

### As a driver,
I want to carpool with other people so that I can get gas money and reduce my overall cost to get to my destination. 🤑

### As a person without a car,
 I want to carpool so that I can get to where I want to go quickly without having to spend an exorbitant amount of money.

### As a rider, 
I want the app to handle the transaction so that I can get in and out seamlessly. 

### As a user on the app, 
I want it to be easy to see where the available drivers are going so that I can pick the best route for me (potential safety/privacy concern)
Perhaps just show the general direction that drivers are going (like how facebook marketplace shows a general area of the sellers)

### As a student, 
I want the driver to also be a student so that it is easier to relate to/interact with them.
